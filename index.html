Communicating Data-driven Policy Research

A proposal by Isabel Meirelles, Reszegh Botond and Csongor Bartus

all-in-one solution / A website which prints.

1. Update freely your online interactive portfolio
  • With a standard Content Management System (CMS)
  • No restrictions on content or design. Add anything you want
2. Generate leaflets, bruchures or presentations. Automatically
  • In your browser select File → Print → Print to file (PDF) ... and that's all.
  • This PDF is ready-made for print, Kindle and e-book.
